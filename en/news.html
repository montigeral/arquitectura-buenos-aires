Cultural News of Buenos Aires

Discover some recent news and events related to the architecture, cultural heritage, and artistic activities of the city of Buenos Aires.

2026 International Architecture Biennial

Buenos Aires is preparing a new edition of the International Architecture Biennial, an event that brings together architects, urban planners, and designers from different countries to discuss the future of cities and sustainable urban development.

The central exhibition will focus on the concept of "Inhabiting" and will feature innovative projects that reflect on the relationship between architecture, society, and the environment.

Night of the Museums

The city of Buenos Aires celebrates the Night of the Museums every year, a cultural event where museums, cultural centers, and historic buildings open their doors to the public for free throughout the night.

The activity includes exhibitions, concerts, workshops, and guided tours of historic spaces that allow you to discover the cultural and architectural wealth of the city.

Read more about the event

Heritage Week

Heritage Week invites you to discover historic buildings, palaces, and museums in Buenos Aires through guided tours, workshops, and talks on architectural conservation.

During this event, you can freely tour more than 40 emblematic buildings and learn about their history, architecture, and cultural value within the city's urban identity.

Fairs and Exhibitions

The International Book Fair celebrates its 50th edition

The Buenos Aires Book Fair will celebrate a special edition for its 50th anniversary. This event brings together writers, publishers, readers, and artists every year in one of the most important cultural gatherings in Latin America.

The edition will include exhibitions, shows, and new interactive experiences to attract different audiences and strengthen international cultural exchange.

Read full news

ARTEBA: international contemporary art fair

ARTEBA is one of the most important contemporary art fairs in Latin America. The event gathers galleries, artists, and collectors from all over the world and promotes the development of the Argentine art market.

In addition to modern and contemporary art exhibitions, the fair includes talks, educational programs, and spaces dedicated to emerging new talents.

Learn more

First Comic Biennial in Buenos Aires

The city announced the realization of the first Comic Biennial, an event dedicated to celebrating comics as cultural heritage and artistic expression.

The biennial will bring together illustrators, scriptwriters, and publishers to show the evolution of Argentine comics, internationally recognized for their narrative and aesthetic innovation.
